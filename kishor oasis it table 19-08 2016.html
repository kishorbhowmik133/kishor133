Syllabus for B.Sc.Engg.(APPE),Session:2015-2016

The courses designed for B.Sc.Engg.(APEE) Degree in the Applied Physics & Electronic Engineering (APEE) consist of 4000 marks of 160 credits distributed two semesters in four acdemic years. Each academic year is divided into two semesters (odd and even) each of duration not less than 11 weeks (66 working days).There shall be final examinations at end the each semester.The medium of answer in all examination will be English. The theoretical examination of courses less than equal to 2 credits shall be of 2 hours duration and courses greater than 2 credits shall be of 3 hours duration.An academic schedule for an academic years shall be announced for general notification before the start of the academic year,on the prior approval of the Academic Committee. No student shall ordinarily be admitted after the class starts.
